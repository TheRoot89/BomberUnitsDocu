3 Level Management Component
4 ==========================
5 
6 constitutes scripts taking care of scene changes and of describing the levels
7 themselves. Levels have to be registered in a list here to appear on the level selection pane.
8 
9 The level::LevelManager connects mainly to the net::NetAdapter to sync scene loading and level selection.<br>
10 The level::Tile class is centerpiece of the level tile-based graph-building, see \ref page_comp_tile_system.
11 The tile system is mainly used by game mechanics, e.g. to determine the area-of-effect of an explosion.
