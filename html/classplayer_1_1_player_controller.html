Detailed Description

Is responsible for everything related to the player figure.

The following topics are delegated to handling classes:

  • Input Handling and character movement: InputHandler
  • Customization of character looks: CharCustomizer
  • Bomb handling: holding, placing, throwing and kicking: BombHandler
